Campus Life

School Church

Was Established in 1883

Star of the Sea College is a Catholic school for girls. This is the church were mass is held every once a fortnight and some extras for special events.



Senior Campus

Was Established in 1883

This area is mainly for the senior students of the school to spend during snack and lunch time. It is a calm and easy area for the students to rest and hang out with one another.



Performing arts room

Was Established in 1883

The performing arts room is a class students do for a few years, it helps them express themselves by acting and dressing up. It is a great way for students to make friends by being grouped up into pairs or small groups for warm ups and the activites.

School Achievements

Stacella
25 year aniversary of Stacella

Stacella is an inspiring choral program unique to Star of the Sea College. Stacella has become well-known throughout Australia for its choral prowess, performing at Eisteddfods, the Australian A Cappella Championships (champions in 2018 and 2019), the Time to Shine Gala, Stacella Concert, as well as at numerous school functions, concerts and liturgies.

House Music Festival
Stella came first this year for the music festival

Star House Music Festival is a great friendly competition with the 4 house groups of star (Stella, Ave, Nagle and Fay) with the winning house to be Stella, who won every catagorey! It is a great way for students to be able to sing, dance and be in a band with a large group of people and compete againts thier piers. Not only that but students get to dress up, pick their music, help create banners and designs all for the Music Festival. I 100% recommend to go watch it, it is amazing.

Swimming Carnival
The Swimming Carnival that's full of fun

The Swimming Carnival is great fun for competition! You are able to try out for all the events you want and it isn't just swimming, you can also sign up for diving! If none of that sounds very fun to you instead you can just dress up in your house colours and house theme and cheer on your classmates during the races! It's a great day to compete, cheer and dress up, but you will probably loose your voice by the end of the day!
